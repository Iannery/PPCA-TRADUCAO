Research Topics

Data Science

With seven professors, it encompasses research in the following topics: database systems, data or text mining, artificial intelligence, and computational intelligence (genetic algorithms, fuzzy logic, neural networks, deep learning.

 

Software Engineering

With eight professors, it covers research in the following topics: algorithms and data structures, object-oriented analysis and programming, requirements engineering, software design, software construction, software testing and validation, software project management, database systems, data or text mining, software development using artificial intelligence techniques, and information security.

 

Risk Management

It includes seven professors and covers research in the following topics: governance and risk management, project risk management, risk management of physical and financial assets, operations research, decision-making models, and performance management.

 

Reasons for these lines of research

It is well known that Brasília is Brazil's second-largest IT buyer market. However, its IT infrastructure management, in general, is lacking. Another common problem faced is the absence of methodologies for software development and testing since the demand for new applications imposes short deadlines for development. It is not uncommon to develop applications without using development methodologies that add to the developed application characteristics of correctness, security, and maintainability. The IT management of today's institutions is highly complex due to the high specialization of the professionals in this area and the extreme dependence that these institutions have on the continuity of the services provided. Possible service interruptions, especially in the access to the world wide web or even in critical systems such as database servers, can provoke strong reactions from the users. In this complex context, it is desirable that the current manager has good notions of risk management to map weaknesses, whether in management processes, the infrastructure itself, or the applications developed, in order to plan mitigating actions or even contingency plans.
